Chassis

Engineering notebook + knowledge log

The build log your whole team can actually read.

Chassis is a shared notebook for competitive robotics teams. Log a fix, a decision, or a dead end, tag it, and the next person who hits the same wall finds your notes in seconds instead of rediscovering them the hard way.

Every entry is searchable, taggable, and linkable. Mark problems Solved, pin the ones that matter, and export the whole thing as a judged-ready notebook PDF. Built for FLL, FTC, FRC, and VEX.

Log it once

Write down the gearbox fix the day you make it. Tag it Drivetrain. Anyone on the team can correct or extend it later.

Find it fast

Fuzzy search forgives typos. Searching gearbx still surfaces the gearbox entry. Filter by tag and status at the same time.

Share and submit

Generate a read-only link for another team, or export an Engineering Notebook PDF for judging. One click each.
